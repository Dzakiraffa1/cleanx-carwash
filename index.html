FAQ

  • What should customers prepare for car wash services?

    For car washing services, the equipment that must be provided by the customer is water, a hose, a bucket and also an electrical panel for the vacuum cleaner. Customers are also asked to ensure that the car wash area is spacious, shaded and not exposed to direct sunlight.

  • Can all cars be dry washed/minimally watered?

    Washing with dry wash/minimal water can be done if the car parts are not exposed to mud or soil.

  • Is car fogging fluid safe for humans?

    The liquid contains Quaternary Ammonium which is safe for humans.
